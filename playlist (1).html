The playlist is simply the list of all tracks available on the phone.

Just click on the sorter options(Track, Artist, Album or Search all ) to sort the songs.

The search bar works with the sorter:
"Tracks" -> search all title with the keyword
"Album" -> search all album with the keyword
"Artist" -> search all artist with the keyword
Click "X" to clear the search
Click "Ok" to confirm search
