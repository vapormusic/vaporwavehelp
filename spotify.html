Steps to use the Spotify mode:


1. Install the Spotify app from the Play Store (please "modded premium" versions will not work as Spotify servers can detect those)

2. Select "Mode"  → "Spotify mode" in the Vaporwave Music Player app

3. Log in with your spotify accounts (this will also allow you to play songs, artists, albums, playlists directly from the app.)

4. Voila!

5. If you want to disable Spotify mode , click "Mode"  → "Spotify mode" again.

P.S: more detailed Spotify features will arrive soon
